Who We Are

EarthGuard Alliance is a volunteer-led NGO focused on reducing single-use plastic and restoring coastal environments. We organize local cleanups, run educational workshops, and partner with local businesses and councils to implement better waste practices.

Our Goals

  • Reduce plastic pollution through community action
  • Educate schools and businesses about sustainable choices
  • Support local policies that improve waste management

Our Approach

We combine practical action (cleanup events and drop-off programs) with awareness campaigns and policy advocacy. We measure success through reduced litter counts, volunteer participation and policy wins.
